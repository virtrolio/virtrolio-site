OVERVIEW

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit https://virtrolio.web.app (the “Site”).

DEVICE INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

  • “Cookies” are data files that are placed on your device or computer and often include an anonymous unique identifier. For more information about cookies, and how to disable cookies, visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org.
  • “Log files” track actions occurring on the Site, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.
  • “Web beacons,” “tags,” and “pixels” are electronic files used to record information about how you browse the Site.
  • Google Analytics also collects device information through us; see SHARING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION for more details.

PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

“Personal Information” refers to any of the information identified in this section. When you sign up or sign in with us we collect certain information from you, including your name, email address, and profile picture.

HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We use your Personal Information in order to keep track of what email addresses have an account with us, what email addresses have signed others’ virtrolios, and what name and profile picture to display when someone reads your message, among other applications.

We use the Device Information that we collect to help us screen for potential risk and fraud (in particular, your IP address), and more generally to improve and optimize our Site (for example, by generating analytics about how our customers browse and interact with the Site, and to assess the effectiveness of our marketing and advertising campaigns).

DATA OWNERSHIP & RETENTION

Our data ownership policies are designed to emulate real-life yearbook signings. By using our Service, you agree to your message text, message attributes and any attached images (collectively referred to hereafter as your “messages” or a “message”) being governed by the following policy.

Once you (the “sender”) send a message, you will no longer be able to modify your message, delete it, or save it, so please do so beforehand. By sending a message through our service to a person (the “receiver”), you agree to transfer ownership of the data in your message to that person. If you, the sender, delete your Virtrolio account, your message data will remain on our servers under the ownership of the receiver unless deleted directly by us or by the receiver. Your content will not be publicly displayed or otherwise distributed by us but may be viewed by administrators and developers in order to provide and develop a better service.

By receiving a message, you gain control over certain, but not all, aspects of that message. The receiver is the only user with the ability to delete a message (that they have received), and if a receiver chooses to delete their Virtrolio account and all their data, all messages they have received from other users will also be deleted. Modification of received messages is neither offered nor allowed by our platform.

If you, the sender, create, upload, or otherwise disseminate content through Virtrolio that is deemed not to comply with our Terms or relevant policies and laws, the onus for the act remains on you regardless of the transfer of data ownership. The contents of a message are still associated with your account even after it has been sent to a receiver, and any investigation, inquiry, and consequences will be directed towards you, the sender. Receivers have the ability and right to flag or report a message they have received.
