Charity

COVID-19 has affected people around the world and it has created problems far more pressing than an absence of yearbook signing. With this in mind, Virtrolio has decided to donate 5 cents for each of the first 2000 Virtrolio accounts created between June 15th, 2021 and June 29th, 2021 to the COVID-19 Solidarity Response Fund. We personally guarantee that we will pay the full amount for the first 2000 accounts created in this period and if funds are available, we will try to match this amount for the next 1000 accounts. Thank you for your support.
